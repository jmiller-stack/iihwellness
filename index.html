About

A safe space to do the hard work

I'm Alexcia Wattie, a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW-C) based in Maryland with years of experience supporting individuals, couples, and families through some of life's most challenging moments.

My approach is warm, direct, and collaborative. I draw from evidence-based practices and adapt my style to meet each client where they are — not where I think they should be.

I've worked with clients ages 16 and up, across a wide range of backgrounds and experiences. Whether you're dealing with anxiety that won't let up, trauma from the past, or relationship dynamics that keep cycling — I'm here to help.
